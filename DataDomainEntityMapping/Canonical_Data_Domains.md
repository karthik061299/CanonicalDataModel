# Canonical Data Domains

## Overview
This document defines 25 canonical data domains that are mutually exclusive and collectively exhaustive for the Financial Institute's enterprise data architecture.

## Canonical Data Domains

| Serial Number | Data Domain Name | Definition |
|---------------|------------------|------------|
| 1 | Customer | Data related to individual and corporate customers, including personal information, demographics, relationships, and customer lifecycle management. Encompasses all customer identification, profiling, and relationship data across retail, commercial, and institutional segments. |
| 2 | Account | Data on financial accounts, balances, account types, and account management operations. Includes deposit accounts, investment accounts, loan accounts, and all account-related transactions and maintenance activities. |
| 3 | Transaction | Data related to all financial transactions, payments, transfers, and monetary movements. Encompasses transaction processing, settlement, clearing, and all forms of payment activities including ACH, wire, check, and electronic payments. |
| 4 | Loan | Data encompassing all lending products, loan origination, servicing, and portfolio management. Includes mortgage loans, personal loans, commercial loans, credit facilities, and all loan lifecycle management from application to payoff. |
| 5 | Credit | Data related to credit assessment, credit scoring, risk evaluation, and creditworthiness determination. Encompasses credit reports, credit ratings, risk models, and all credit-related analytics and decisioning processes. |
| 6 | Product | Data defining financial products, services, pricing, features, and product catalog management. Includes product specifications, terms, conditions, eligibility criteria, and product lifecycle management across all business lines. |
| 7 | Investment | Data related to securities, investments, portfolio management, and wealth management services. Encompasses investment accounts, securities positions, trading activities, and investment advisory services including mutual funds and brokerage operations. |
| 8 | Compliance | Data supporting regulatory compliance, risk management, and governance activities. Includes regulatory reporting, audit trails, compliance monitoring, policy management, and all regulatory requirement fulfillment across banking regulations. |
| 9 | Employee | Data related to human resources, employee management, and organizational structure. Encompasses employee records, compensation, benefits, performance management, training, and all workforce-related information and processes. |
| 10 | Document | Data related to document management, content storage, and information governance. Includes loan documents, contracts, agreements, policies, procedures, and all document lifecycle management including archival and retention. |
| 11 | Marketing | Data supporting marketing campaigns, customer acquisition, and customer experience management. Encompasses campaign management, lead generation, customer segmentation, marketing analytics, and customer journey orchestration. |
| 12 | Channel | Data related to customer interaction channels, digital banking platforms, and service delivery mechanisms. Includes online banking, mobile banking, branch operations, call center, and all customer touchpoint management. |
| 13 | Fraud | Data supporting fraud detection, prevention, and investigation activities. Encompasses fraud alerts, suspicious activity monitoring, investigation cases, fraud patterns, and all anti-fraud measures and controls. |
| 14 | Clearing | Data related to securities clearing, settlement operations, and custody services. Includes trade settlement, clearing house operations, securities custody, and all post-trade processing activities for investment services. |
| 15 | Accounting | Data supporting financial accounting, general ledger, and financial reporting operations. Encompasses chart of accounts, journal entries, financial statements, budgeting, and all accounting and finance management activities. |
| 16 | Legal | Data related to legal matters, litigation management, and legal compliance. Includes legal cases, contracts, regulatory matters, court proceedings, and all legal risk management and litigation support activities. |
| 17 | Technology | Data supporting IT operations, system management, and technology infrastructure. Encompasses system configurations, technical metadata, IT assets, application management, and all technology operations and support activities. |
| 18 | Reference | Data providing reference information, lookup tables, and master data management. Includes codes, classifications, geographic data, industry standards, and all reference data supporting business operations and data consistency. |
| 19 | Analytics | Data supporting business intelligence, reporting, and analytical processing. Encompasses data marts, metrics, KPIs, dashboards, and all analytical data structures supporting decision-making and performance management. |
| 20 | Vendor | Data related to third-party vendors, suppliers, and external service providers. Includes vendor management, procurement, contracts, vendor risk assessment, and all external relationship management activities. |
| 21 | Security | Data supporting information security, access control, and cybersecurity operations. Encompasses user authentication, authorization, security policies, incident management, and all security-related controls and monitoring. |
| 22 | Audit | Data supporting internal audit, external audit, and audit trail management. Includes audit findings, recommendations, audit plans, compliance testing, and all audit-related documentation and tracking activities. |
| 23 | Fee | Data related to fee structures, pricing, revenue recognition, and fee management. Encompasses service charges, transaction fees, account fees, and all fee-related calculations, billing, and revenue management activities. |
| 24 | Insurance | Data related to insurance products, claims, and insurance operations. Includes insurance policies, coverage, claims processing, underwriting, and all insurance-related activities for both bank-offered and required insurance products. |
| 25 | Workflow | Data supporting business process management, workflow automation, and operational procedures. Encompasses process definitions, task management, approvals, escalations, and all workflow orchestration and business process execution. |
