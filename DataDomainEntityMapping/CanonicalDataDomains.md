# Canonical Data Domains

Serial Number | Data Domain Name | Definition
--- | --- | ---
1 | Customer | Data related to individuals and organizations interacting with the bank, including identification, segmentation, and contact details.
2 | Account | Data on financial accounts, balances, account types, and account relationships across all products.
3 | Product | Data describing banking, investment, insurance, and ancillary products and services offered.
4 | Transaction | Data about financial and non-financial transactions, including payments, transfers, and operational events.
5 | Loan | Data on loan products, applications, approvals, servicing, and repayments.
6 | Collateral | Data about assets pledged as security for loans and credit facilities.
7 | Credit | Data related to credit scoring, credit decisions, risk assessment, and credit attributes.
8 | Payment | Data for payment instructions, processing, clearing, settlement, and payment history.
9 | Deposit | Data on deposit accounts, deposit transactions, and associated balances.
10 | Security | Data on securities, investments, holdings, and related trade and settlement information.
11 | Trade | Data on trade execution, clearing, settlement, and trade lifecycle events.
12 | Compliance | Data for regulatory reporting, risk management, AML, KYC, and policy adherence.
13 | Risk | Data on risk metrics, risk models, stress tests, and risk mitigation activities.
14 | Policy | Data on business, regulatory, and operational policies and controls.
15 | Employee | Data related to employees, HR, positions, compensation, and performance.
16 | Organization | Data about business units, departments, legal entities, and organizational structure.
17 | Legal | Data on legal matters, cases, contracts, filings, and regulatory actions.
18 | Marketing | Data for campaigns, segments, customer journeys, and marketing analytics.
19 | Document | Data about documents, contracts, disclosures, and document management.
20 | Asset | Data on physical and financial assets, asset management, and valuations.
21 | Portfolio | Data on collections of accounts, loans, investments, and portfolio performance.
22 | System | Data about source systems, applications, configuration, and integration endpoints.
23 | Process | Data on business processes, workflows, tasks, and process metrics.
24 | Report | Data for operational, regulatory, and analytical reporting and dashboards.
25 | Event | Data on business, system, and customer events, including alerts, notifications, and audit logs.
