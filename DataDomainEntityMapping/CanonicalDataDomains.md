# Canonical Data Domains

| Serial Number | Data Domain Name           | Definition |
|---------------|---------------------------|------------|
| 1             | Credit Application         | Data capturing credit applications, their decisioning, approval processes, and policy exceptions. Includes workflow and underwriting steps for loan origination. |
| 2             | Borrower/Customer         | Data related to individuals or organizations applying for credit, including identity, contact, and demographic information. |
| 3             | Credit Score & Risk       | Data representing credit scores, risk ratings, risk models, predictive analytics, and factors influencing creditworthiness. System-agnostic measures for risk assessment. |
| 4             | Credit Limit & Exposure   | Data about credit limits, account balances, exposure, utilization, and attributes of credit accounts. Covers financial exposure and account status. |
| 5             | Collateral & Asset        | Data on assets pledged against credit, including collateral valuation, property, and vehicle records. |
| 6             | Income & Financials       | Data on borrower income, debt-to-income ratios, loan-to-value calculations, and financial verification. |
| 7             | Credit History            | Data on historical credit performance, payment history, delinquency, public records, and negative credit events. Includes bankruptcy, foreclosure, and collection records. |
| 8             | Rating & Analytics        | Data on credit ratings, rating agencies, rating actions, peer comparisons, sector/industry analytics, and economic indicators. Used for benchmarking and reporting. |
| 9             | Compliance & Fraud        | Data supporting regulatory compliance, fraud prevention, identity verification, document authentication, and security alerts. |
| 10            | Monitoring & Dispute      | Data for ongoing credit monitoring, dispute management, and resolution of credit report discrepancies. |
| 11            | Decision Models           | Data on scoring models, predictive models, validation, simulation, and strategy for automated decision-making. |
| 12            | Source System Reference   | System-specific reference data, codes, and mappings supporting harmonization across platforms. |
| 13            | Public Record             | Data from public sources including liens, judgments, bankruptcy filings, and other legal records impacting credit. |
| 14            | Portfolio Analytics       | Data for analyzing credit portfolios, benchmarking, and predictive insights across multiple entities and sectors. |
| 15            | Entity Relationships      | Data capturing relationships and networks among borrowers, co-signers, and associated parties. |
| 16            | Security & Alerts         | Data on security freezes, alerts, and fraud indicators for consumer protection. |
| 17            | Asset Verification        | Data used to verify assets, ownership, and authenticity for underwriting and risk management. |
| 18            | Economic Indicators       | Data on macroeconomic variables, market trends, and sector performance affecting credit risk. |
| 19            | Dispute Management        | Data supporting dispute resolution, tracking, and audit trails for credit report corrections. |
| 20            | Compliance Reporting      | Data for regulatory reporting, compliance checks, and audit management. |
| 21            | Underwriting Policy       | Data on credit policies, underwriting rules, and exceptions for credit decisioning. |
| 22            | Approval Authority        | Data on approval hierarchies, manual reviews, and authority levels for credit decisions. |
| 23            | Score Distribution        | Data on distribution, trends, and performance of credit scores across populations. |
| 24            | Industry & Sector Codes   | Reference data for industry, sector, and portfolio categorization. |
| 25            | Model Performance         | Data on model validation, performance tracking, and simulation results for risk analytics. |
