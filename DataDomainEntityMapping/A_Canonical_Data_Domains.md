# A. Canonical Data Domains

| Serial Number | Data Domain Name | Definition |
|---------------|------------------|------------|
| 1 | Customer | Data related to individual and corporate customers, including personal information, demographics, relationships, and customer lifecycle management across all business lines. |
| 2 | Account | Data on financial accounts including deposits, savings, checking, investment accounts, balances, account types, and account management operations. |
| 3 | Loan | Data encompassing all lending products including mortgages, personal loans, commercial loans, credit facilities, loan origination, servicing, and portfolio management. |
| 4 | Transaction | Data related to all financial transactions including payments, transfers, deposits, withdrawals, trade executions, and transaction processing across all channels. |
| 5 | Product | Data defining financial products and services offered including product specifications, pricing, terms, conditions, and product catalog management. |
| 6 | Security | Data related to investment securities, instruments, positions, holdings, market data, and securities master information for trading and investment operations. |
| 7 | Payment | Data encompassing payment processing, bill pay, person-to-person payments, ACH, wire transfers, and payment method management across all channels. |
| 8 | Risk | Data related to credit risk, operational risk, market risk, compliance risk, risk assessment, monitoring, and risk management across all business areas. |
| 9 | Compliance | Data supporting regulatory compliance, anti-money laundering, fraud detection, suspicious activity reporting, and regulatory reporting requirements. |
| 10 | Employee | Data related to human resources including employee records, organizational structure, compensation, benefits, performance, and workforce management. |
| 11 | Vendor | Data encompassing third-party vendors, suppliers, service providers, vendor management, contracts, and vendor risk assessment processes. |
| 12 | Document | Data related to document management, storage, retrieval, version control, digital signatures, and document workflow across all business processes. |
| 13 | Marketing | Data supporting marketing campaigns, customer segmentation, lead generation, campaign management, and marketing analytics across all channels. |
| 14 | Channel | Data related to delivery channels including online banking, mobile banking, branch operations, ATM networks, and customer interaction touchpoints. |
| 15 | Clearing | Data encompassing securities clearing, settlement, custody operations, trade processing, and post-trade services for broker-dealer operations. |
| 16 | Collateral | Data related to loan collateral, asset valuation, collateral management, pledging, monitoring, and collateral risk assessment processes. |
| 17 | Fee | Data encompassing all fee structures, service charges, commissions, pricing models, fee calculations, and revenue recognition across products. |
| 18 | Interest | Data related to interest rates, rate management, interest calculations, yield curves, rate risk management, and interest income/expense tracking. |
| 19 | Reporting | Data supporting business intelligence, regulatory reporting, financial reporting, analytics, dashboards, and management information systems. |
| 20 | Reference | Data encompassing reference data management including codes, lookups, master data, data dictionaries, and system configuration parameters. |
| 21 | Legal | Data related to legal matters, litigation, contracts, legal compliance, court cases, legal documentation, and legal risk management. |
| 22 | Accounting | Data supporting general ledger, financial accounting, budgeting, cost accounting, financial statements, and accounting operations. |
| 23 | Technology | Data related to IT systems, applications, infrastructure, data management, system integration, and technology operations support. |
| 24 | Audit | Data encompassing internal audit, external audit, audit trails, audit findings, compliance testing, and audit management processes. |
| 25 | Insurance | Data related to insurance products, policies, claims, coverage, premiums, and insurance operations for both customer products and operational coverage. |
